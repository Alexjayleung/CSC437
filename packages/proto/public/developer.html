Developer: Riot Games

Riot Games is a game developer and publisher based in Los Angeles, California. Founded in 2006, Riot Games is best known for its flagship game, League of Legends, which has become one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games in the world.

Key Information

  • Name: Riot Games
  • Founded: September 2006
  • Location: Los Angeles, California

Notable Games

  • Valorant
  • Legends of Runeterra
  • Teamfight Tactics
